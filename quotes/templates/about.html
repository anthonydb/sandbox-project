About

Words on Art is site by Anthony DeBarros that collects the words of musicians, writers, journalists, poets, painters and other artistic types to inspire creativity and remind us of beauty.

The site runs on the Django web framework. Words are displayed using Lusitana from the Google Web Fonts API.
